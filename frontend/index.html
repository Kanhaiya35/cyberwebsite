About Our Platform

Empowering citizens to fight cybercrime together

What is CyberSafe Portal?

CyberSafe Portal is a comprehensive public cybercrime reporting and tracking system designed to provide citizens with a secure, efficient way to report cyber incidents and monitor their case progress.

Our platform bridges the gap between victims and law enforcement, ensuring transparency, accountability, and timely resolution of cybercrimes.

  • Easy-to-use reporting interface
  • Real-time case tracking with unique tokens
  • Dedicated admin dashboard for case management
  • Complete data privacy and security
